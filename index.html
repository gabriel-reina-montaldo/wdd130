Montevideo, Uruguay

Montevideo is the capital and largest city of Uruguay, located on the southern coast along the Río de la Plata. Known for its laid-back atmosphere, colonial architecture, and beautiful coastline, the city blends modern life with rich cultural traditions. Montevideo is also known because of the art, music and football atmosphere, offering a unique South American charm.
